About

Abheshek Pandey (he/him) is a recent graduate with a BEng (hons) in Aerospace Engineering, Astronautics and Space Technology from Kingston University, London. His thesis project involved researching biodegradable alternatives for composites used in aircrafts to make them greener. Ironically, he does not like the colour green.

He is currently based in Mumbai, India, where he is a CAD & CAM Technician. Working either independently or collaboratively, he supports the efficient running of operations through his experience in project management, and effective communication and time management.

He is expanding his skill-set to include a greater knowledge of programming languages, such as Python, R & SQL, and is exploring Web Design as a medium for his other creative ventures. He is currently tinkerings with Pure Data, JavaScript and Web Audio API to produce algorithmic and generative computer music.

His hobbies include hiking and , which often go hand in hand, unlike his interest in reading books and watching movies. He can never seem to them together. He writes fiction under the pen name Ayan De.
